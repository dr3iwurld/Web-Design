About Me

James Andrei C. Cruz

Hi! I’m Andrei, a Multimedia Arts student from FEU Institute of Technology, living in Marilao, Bulacan. I like video editing, illustration, and design, and I’ve been getting curious about 3D modeling too. Most of the time, I’m either making music or just staring at the sky thinking about random stuff. I don’t really work on my craft as much as I should, but I have my interests and dreams, maybe to freelance, work as a designer, or be part of a film crew someday.
